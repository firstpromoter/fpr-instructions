# Adding FirstPromoter to your Ghost Website

The integration between FirstPromoter and Ghost is fully automated and does not require any additional scripts to be added.

## Setting things up

To set up the integration, you need to:

1.  **Copy your FirstPromoter Account ID**: After signing up for FirstPromoter, you can find your Account ID in the Settings menu under the Integrations tab.
2.  **Paste the ID into Ghost**: In your Ghost Admin settings, navigate to the Integrations page, enable the FirstPromoter integration, and paste your Account ID.
3.  **Launch your program**: Once you save the changes, you can invite members to promote your work in exchange for rewards.
